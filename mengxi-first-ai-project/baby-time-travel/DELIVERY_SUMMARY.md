## 🌟 Educational Value

### Language Development Insights (Woven into Dialogue):
- **Prenatal hearing** - Babies hear voices in the womb (~week 18-24)
- **Voice recognition** - Newborns recognize mom's voice immediately
- **Social smiling** - Real smiles emerge around 3 months
- **Babbling** - "Ba-ba-ba" starts around 6 months
- **First words** - Typically around 12 months
- **Responsive conversation** - Builds neural connections

## 💡 Key Innovations

### 1. **Time-Travel Narrative**
Unlike the Pudding article (strangers meeting now), this features the **same relationship across time** - a mother talking to her bump, and the baby responding from the future.

### 2. **Educational Storytelling**
Subtly teaches language development without being didactic. Parents learn through experiencing relatable moments.

### 3. **Emotional Arc**
From anxiety ("What if I mess up?") to reassurance ("You're going to be amazing") to celebration ("Happy birthday!").

### 4. **First-Person Future**
Baby Emma speaks in first-person from the future, creating an intimate, magical connection.
