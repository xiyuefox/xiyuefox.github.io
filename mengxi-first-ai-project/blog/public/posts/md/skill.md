# Educational Prompts Skill

## 目的

Provide easy access to Wharton Business School's professionally designed educational prompts for AI-assisted teaching and learning.

## 概述

This skill offers 17 categorized educational prompts developed by Wharton Business School professors. These prompts are designed to help educators create engaging learning experiences, assessments, and instructional materials using AI.

## 可用提示类别

1. Simulation Creator - GPT4 and Gemini Advanced
2. Project Ideas for Class - GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Anthropic’s Claude
3. Quiz Creator – GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Claude, and Bing Chat in Creative Mode
4. Active learning co-creator - GPT4 and Claude
5. Syllabus co-creator - GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Claude, Bing
6. Co-develop an explanation for any topic - GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Bing
7. Structured Prompt Designer - GPT4
8. Lesson Crafter - GPT4, Claude, Gemini Advanced
9. General Tutor - GPT4
10. AI Mentor Gives Feedback - GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Claude, Bing
11. AI Student (Student evaluates AI output and teaches the AI) - GPT4, Claude, Bing, Gemini Advanced
12. Negotiation Simulator - GPT4, Claude 3, Gemini 1.5, Gemini Advanced
13. Team After Action Review - GPT4, Claude, Gemini Advanced
14. Team Charter - GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Claude, Bing
15. Class Reflection Aid - GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Claude
16. Devil's Advocate - GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Claude, Bing
17. Team Premortem - GPT4, Gemini Advanced, Claude, Bing

## 指令

### 步骤 1: 响应用户请求
当用户询问教育提示或上述任何用例时，列出所有可用的提示类别。

### 步骤 2: 检索指定提示
当用户选择一个特定的提示类别时，使用 Grep 或 Read 工具从 references/prompts.md 文件中检索对应的提示内容。

### 步骤 3: 提供使用指导
将检索到的提示内容呈现给用户，并提供关于如何在 AI 平台上使用该提示的清晰说明。

## 输出格式

- 首先呈现完整的提示内容
- 然后添加使用说明和注意事项
- 如果需要，提供示例使用场景

## 错误处理

如果无法找到指定的提示类别：
1. 向用户道歉
2. 再次列出所有可用类别
3. 询问用户是否需要其他帮助
