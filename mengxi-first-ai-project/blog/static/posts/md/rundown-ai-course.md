# Chapter 3: Google NotebookLM  
第 3 章：Google NotebookLM

**Welcome to Chapter 3!** In this lesson, we'll explore how to supercharge your notetaking with Google’s new AI-powered notetaker, [NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google/?utm_source=www.therundown.ai&utm_medium=referral)[.](https://copilot.microsoft.com/?utm_source=www.therundown.ai&utm_medium=referral)  
欢迎来到第 3 章！在本课程中，我们将探索如何使用 Google 全新的人工智能笔记工具 NotebookLM 来增强你​​的笔记功能。

![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/c63ae3d1-a489-4b9e-ac68-bf9f082cd9fa/hq720.jpg?t=1705286234)

**By the end of this chapter, you'll be able to:  
在本章结束时，您将能够：**

- Access NotebookLM for free  
    免费访问 NotebookLM
    
- Know how to use AI in your existing Google Documents  
    了解如何在现有的 Google 文档中使用 AI
    
- Summarize, take notes, and outline ideas using NotebookLM  
    使用 NotebookLM 总结、记笔记和概述想法
    

Let’s get into it. 让我们开始吧。

### What is Google NotebookLM?  
什么是 Google NotebookLM？

While Microsoft and OpenAI get a lot of hype with ChatGPT and Copilot, but Google has one thing that Microsoft doesn’t have: An AI-powered notetaker.  
虽然微软和 OpenAI 凭借 ChatGPT 和 Copilot 获得了大量炒作，但谷歌拥有微软所没有的东西：人工智能驱动的记事本。

Google’s AI notetaker is called NotebookLM, and provides unique productivity benefits:  
Google 的 AI 记事本称为 NotebookLM，可提供独特的生产力优势：

- It connects directly to your Google Docs and scans them to understand your work.  
    它直接连接到您的 Google 文档并扫描它们以了解您的工作。
    
- Using this knowledge, NotebookLM can summarize notes, answer questions, and help organize your documents like a personalized AI assistant.  
    利用这些知识，NotebookLM 可以像个性化 AI 助手一样总结笔记、回答问题并帮助组织文档。
    
- This makes it great for writers, researchers, students or anyone who wants to boost efficiency.  
    这对于作家、研究人员、学生或任何想要提高效率的人来说非常有用。
    
- It’s completely free.  它是完全免费的。
    

NotebookLM uses Google's latest natural language AI called Gemini to analyze text and collaborate with you on documents. It's like having an AI note-taking expert at your fingertips.  
NotebookLM 使用 Google 最新的自然语言 AI（称为 Gemini）来分析文本并与您协作处理文档。这就像触手可及的人工智能笔记专家。

The key is that NotebookLM integrates directly with Docs rather than being a separate tool. This makes it easy to enhance existing workflows with AI.  
关键是 NotebookLM 直接与 Docs 集成，而不是作为一个单独的工具。这使得利用人工智能增强现有工作流程变得很容易。

Let's look at how to access NotebookLM's capabilities and start supercharging your productivity.  
让我们看看如何访问 NotebookLM 的功能并开始提高您的工作效率。

### How To Access NotebookLM 如何访问 NotebookLM

If you're located in the United States, you can sign up and get started with NotebookLM directly using this link: [notebooklm.google/signup](https://notebooklm.google/signup?utm_source=www.therundown.ai&utm_medium=referral).  
如果您位于美国，则可以使用以下链接直接注册并开始使用 NotebookLM：notebooklm.google/signup。

However, things get a tad trickier if you're outside the U.S. At the time of writing this chapter, NotebookLM is only available in the U.S. (but it’ll expand to other countries shortly).  
然而，如果您在美国境外，事情就会变得有点棘手。在撰写本章时，NotebookLM 仅在美国可用（但很快就会扩展到其他国家/地区）。

A simple workaround is to use a free VPN browser extension to access NotebookLM, which we detail below.  
一个简单的解决方法是使用免费的 VPN 浏览器扩展来访问 NotebookLM，我们将在下面详细介绍。

![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/5e0fad3e-a69e-4ab3-9968-1a81e530041c/GBFKf5DX0AABjJQ__1_.jpeg?t=1705288452)

### Download A Free VPN (If Outside The U.S.)  
下载免费 VPN（如果在美国境外）

There are a few really good installable VPNs like Surfshark, NordVPN, or Proton, but for the sake of saving time (and money), we’re going to opt for a free browser option.  
有一些非常好的可安装 VPN，例如 Surfshark、NordVPN 或 Proton，但为了节省时间（和金钱），我们将选择免费的浏览器选项。

Simply download a browser app with a built-in VPN such as [Brave](https://brave.com/firewall-vpn/?utm_source=www.therundown.ai&utm_medium=referral), [Opera](https://www.opera.com/features/free-vpn?utm_source=www.therundown.ai&utm_medium=referral), or [Arc](https://arc.net/?utm_source=www.therundown.ai&utm_medium=referral), enable the VPN, set your location to the United States, and you’ll be ready to use Google NotebookLM in about 30 seconds.  
只需下载带有内置 VPN 的浏览器应用程序（例如 Brave、Opera 或 Arc），启用 VPN，将您的位置设置为美国，大约 30 秒内您就可以使用 Google NotebookLM。

The VPN allows you to securely tunnel to the US so you can sign up for NotebookLM from anywhere in the world.  
VPN 允许您安全地通过隧道连接到美国，这样您就可以从世界任何地方注册 NotebookLM。

Once your account is created, you can turn off the VPN and continue using NotebookLM as normal. This quick setup gives you free access to NotebookLM's AI-powered productivity features globally.  
创建帐户后，您可以关闭 VPN 并继续正常使用 NotebookLM。通过这种快速设置，您可以在全球范围内免费访问 NotebookLM 的 AI 驱动的生产力功能。

![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/4ce1a2be-2184-4328-9978-fb5327c7e6ea/Screenshot_2024-01-14_at_7.02.31_PM.png?t=1705287760)

### Connect Your Existing Google Docs  
连接您现有的 Google 文档

A great feature of NotebookLM is that you don't need to recreate your notes — you can connect your existing Google Docs.  
NotebookLM 的一个很棒的功能是您无需重新创建笔记 - 您可以连接现有的 Google 文档。

Here's how:  就是这样：

**Step 1:** In NotebookLM, click "New Source"  
第1步：在NotebookLM中，单击“新源”

**Step 2:** Select the Google Docs you want to link to NotebookLM. You can connect as many as you need.  
步骤 2：选择您想要链接到 NotebookLM 的 Google 文档。您可以根据需要连接任意数量的连接。

**Step 3:** NotebookLM will automatically scan and summarize your selected Docs by generating a helpful "Source Guide."  
第 3 步：NotebookLM 将通过生成有用的“源指南”来自动扫描和总结您选择的文档。

Connecting your Docs only takes just a few clicks and opens up NotebookLM's capabilities to enhance your existing workflow rather than starting from scratch.  
只需单击几下即可连接您的文档，并打开 NotebookLM 的功能来增强您现有的工作流程，而不是从头开始。

You can think of this Source Guide as an AI-powered table of contents, pulling out key information from your documents. It also enables NotebookLM to better understand your work so it can assist you.  
您可以将此源指南视为人工智能驱动的目录，从文档中提取关键信息。它还使 NotebookLM 能够更好地了解您的工作，从而为您提供帮助。

![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/b300cd60-9314-4fe2-bff1-7d0f851ceacc/Screenshot_2024-01-14_at_7.15.43_PM.png?t=1705288548)

### Ask Questions 问问题

Once your Google Docs are connected, NotebookLM automatically suggests questions in the Source Guide based on analyzing your content.  
连接 Google 文档后，NotebookLM 会根据分析您的内容自动在源代码指南中提出问题。

You can click these auto-generated questions to have NotebookLM instantly answer them using the knowledge from your documents.  
您可以单击这些自动生成的问题，让 NotebookLM 使用文档中的知识立即回答这些问题。

For example, if you've linked research notes on self-driving cars, NotebookLM may ask "What are the benefits of self-driving cars?" and provide a summary answer from your docs.  
例如，如果您链​​接了有关自动驾驶汽车的研究笔记，NotebookLM 可能会问“自动驾驶汽车有什么好处？”并从您的文档中提供摘要答案。

You can also manually type or dictate any questions you have to further query your documents with NotebookLM. Think of it like having an AI research assistant.  
您还可以手动输入或口述任何问题，以使用 NotebookLM 进一步查询文档。可以把它想象成有一个人工智能研究助理。

The key is that linking your existing Docs allows NotebookLM to "read" and understand them so it can answer questions or summarize the content for you - no reformatting needed!  
关键是链接您现有的文档允许 NotebookLM “阅读”并理解它们，以便它可以回答问题或为您总结内容 - 无需重新格式化！

This question-answering capability supercharges your ability to review, learn from, and leverage your own work.  
这种问答能力增强了您回顾、学习和利用自己的工作的能力。

![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/c9f81723-a684-417e-bff4-09a5a8609ca2/Screenshot_2024-01-14_at_7.24.46_PM.png?t=1705289092)

### Change Formats 更改格式

In addition to answering questions, NotebookLM can reformat your collected notes into new structured documents.  
除了回答问题之外，NotebookLM 还可以将您收集的笔记重新格式化为新的结构化文档。

**For example, you can: 例如，您可以：**

- Select some notes and have NotebookLM turn them into a study guide.  
    选择一些笔记并让 NotebookLM 将它们变成学习指南。
    
- Ask NotebookLM to summarize your key insights from a research doc into a blog post draft.  
    请 NotebookLM 将您从研究文档中获得的主要见解总结为博客文章草稿。
    
- Convert meeting notes into a professional summary email to send to colleagues.  
    将会议记录转换为专业摘要电子邮件以发送给同事。
    

**To do this, simply: 为此，只需：**

1. Select the relevant notes in NotebookLM.  
    在 NotebookLM 中选择相关笔记。
    
2. Prompt it to create the desired new format, like "Turn these notes into a presentation."  
    提示它创建所需的新格式，例如“将这些笔记变成演示文稿”。
    
3. NotebookLM will generate the new structured document for you!  
    NotebookLM 将为您生成新的结构化文档！
    

This formatting capability allows you to easily repurpose and share knowledge from your connected Docs in new ways, and automate the way you organize your ideas.  
此格式化功能使您能够以新的方式轻松地重新利用和共享连接的文档中的知识，并自动组织您的想法。

![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/2403eda7-c05d-4c69-881e-b5af8e76cebc/Screenshot_2024-01-14_at_7.26.17_PM.png?t=1705289182)

### Pin quotes 引脚引号

Whenever NotebookLM answers a question using your connected documents, it automatically includes quotes and citations from the original sources.  
每当 NotebookLM 使用连接的文档回答问题时，它都会自动包含原始来源的引用和引文。

You can click on these quotes to instantly jump back to that section in the source Google Doc.  
您可以单击这些引用立即跳回源 Google 文档中的该部分。

This allows you to quickly check the full context of the excerpt NotebookLM referenced.  
这使您可以快速检查引用的 NotebookLM 摘录的完整上下文。

The ability to pinpoint key quotes and passages from your documents helps you:  
精确定位文档中的关键引用和段落的能力可以帮助您：

- Verify that NotebookLM's summaries are accurate to your sources.  
    验证 NotebookLM 的摘要是否准确符合您的来源。
    
- Easily reference important snippets by linking back to the original.  
    通过链接回原始内容，轻松引用重要的片段。
    
- Review key details in context rather than just NotebookLM's paraphrasing.  
    在上下文中查看关键细节，而不仅仅是 NotebookLM 的释义。
    

It's a great feature for ensuring transparency and traceability when using NotebookLM. The linked quotes keep you grounded in your original documents.  
这是确保使用 NotebookLM 时的透明度和可追溯性的一项出色功能。链接的引用使您能够立足于原始文档。

![](https://media.beehiiv.com/cdn-cgi/image/fit=scale-down,format=auto,onerror=redirect,quality=80/uploads/asset/file/73147d80-720a-4c7d-a87f-efee01e86c76/Screenshot_2024-01-14_at_7.28.49_PM.png?t=1705289333)
