Age Stages in Early Childhood

幼儿时期的年龄阶段

Understanding the developmental stages of children ages 0-6, with practical parenting tips.

了解0-6岁儿童的发展阶段，以及实用的育儿技巧。

Three building blocks for all learning stages

所有学习阶段的三个构建块

The Golden Period (0-6 Years)

黄金时期 (0-6岁)

0-6 years old is the golden period of brain development. The brain development of a child aged 0-3 has completed 80%, and at the age of 6, it has completed 90%. The quality of the environment during this period has a decisive impact on the child's life.

0-6岁是大脑发育的黄金时期。0-3岁孩子的大脑发育已经完成80%，到6岁时，已经完成90%。这个时期环境的质量对孩子的一生产生决定性影响。

Practical Tips:

实用提示：

  • Respond to your baby's cries in time to build trust.
  • 及时回应宝宝的哭声，建立信任感。
  • Provide safe and stimulating toys.
  • 提供安全且刺激的玩具。
  • Talk to your child in rich language.
  • 用丰富的语言与孩子交谈。

Parent-Child Interaction: Emotional Connection

亲子互动：情感连接

Emotional connection is the foundation for all healthy relationships and lifelong learning. The quality of the parent-child relationship directly affects the child's emotional intelligence, self-esteem, and ability to trust others.

情感连接是所有健康关系和终身学习的基础。亲子关系的质量直接影响孩子的情商、自尊和信任他人的能力。

Practical Tips:

实用提示：

  • Make eye contact and smile at your baby often.
  • 经常与宝宝眼神交流并微笑。
  • Engage in responsive play.
  • 参与回应式游戏。
  • Use warm and gentle tones when speaking.
  • 说话时使用温暖柔和的语气。

Free Play: Natural Learning

自由玩耍：自然学习

Play is the most important way for young children to learn. Through play, children develop language skills, problem-solving abilities, creativity, and social skills. Play also helps children understand the world around them.

玩耍是幼儿学习的最重要方式。通过玩耍，孩子发展语言技能、解决问题的能力、创造力和社交技能。玩耍还帮助孩子了解周围的世界。

Practical Tips:

实用提示：

  • Provide open-ended toys (blocks, paper, clay).
  • 提供开放式玩具（积木、纸张、黏土）。
  • Let your child take the lead in play.
  • 让孩子在游戏中发挥主导作用。
  • Join in the play without controlling it.
  • 加入游戏但不控制游戏。
