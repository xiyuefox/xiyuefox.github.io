About Mengxi Times - Little Learners Hub

Research-based early learning resources for parents of children ages 0-5.

Our Mission

To provide parents of children ages 0-5 with free, research-based resources that make early learning simple, playful, and accessible. We believe every parent can be their child's best teacher, and every moment can be a learning opportunity.

Our Philosophy

  • Play is the work of childhood
  • Relationships are the foundation of learning
  • Everyday moments are learning opportunities
  • Every child develops at their own unique pace
  • Parents are their child's first and most important teacher
  • Learning should be joyful and pressure-free

Research Approach

All our content is based on evidence from leading child development researchers and organizations, including:

  • Dr. Alison Gopnik (University of California, Berkeley)
  • Dr. Daniel Siegel (UCLA School of Medicine)
  • Dr. Roberta Golinkoff and Dr. Kathy Hirsh-Pasek (Temple University)
  • Dr. John Medina (University of Washington School of Medicine)
  • American Academy of Pediatrics
