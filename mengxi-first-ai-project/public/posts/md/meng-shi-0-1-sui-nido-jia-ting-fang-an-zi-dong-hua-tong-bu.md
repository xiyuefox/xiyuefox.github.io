ℹ️ **信息源 (Source)**: The Kavanaugh Report | 🌟 推荐 85 | 💡 实用 90

🧠 **干货总结 (Bilingual Insight)**:
  - 🇨🇳 **中文**: 为0-1岁Nido阶段选择节日礼物时，重点考虑以下三角：
    ① **客体永久性** (投币盒/可藏物品的球)：锻炼"消失-重现"的认知逻辑（前数学核心）；
    ② **细颗粒动作** (迷你刷子/软积木)：刺激手眼协调与空间分割（几何感知前奏）；
    ③ **安全可咬** (硅胶牙胶环)：双重功能（安抚+感官刺激），零占地；
    避免单一功能、易损坏的塑料玩具，优先选择木质/天然材质的一物多用型。
  - 🇬🇧 **English**: For 0-1s, prioritize stocking stuffers that embed Montessori's core pillars:
    - Object Permanence (e.g., coin boxes, popping balls) to wire early logical sequencing;
    - Fine-motor precision (mini brushes, soft blocks) for spatial cognition;
    - Dual-use sensory tools (silicone teething rings) with zero footprint.
    Rule out single-purpose, fragile plastic items; favor compact, multi-functional wood/silicone designs.
