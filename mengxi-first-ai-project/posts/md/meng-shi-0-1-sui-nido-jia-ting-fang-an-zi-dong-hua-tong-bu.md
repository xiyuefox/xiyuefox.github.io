Design "sound-object pairing games" by playing animal/environmental sounds followed by real objects or images to nurture **object permanence** and **cross-modal sensory integration**. Prioritize **natural soundscapes** (e.g., rain, animal calls) over fantasy narratives to align with infant reality-based learning. Liberate hands for tactile exploration during playback.
