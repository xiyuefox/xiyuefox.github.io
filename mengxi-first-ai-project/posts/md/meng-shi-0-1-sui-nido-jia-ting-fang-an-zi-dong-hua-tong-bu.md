🧠 **干货总结 (Bilingual Insight)**:
  - 🇨🇳 **中文**: 蒙台梭利Nido阶段的核心是成人以「环境架构师」的角色，通过「限制设定」「行为示范」等方式间接引导婴儿发展。关键在于将成人自身打造成「可移动的教具」：如通过简化环境（减少干扰物）让婴儿专注于客体永久性训练（如隐藏-寻找游戏），或用温和的「因果关系示范」（如轻推球让其滚动）培养前逻辑思维。需避免过度干预，让婴儿在安全框架内自由探索。
  - 🇬🇧 **English**: For 0-12 months Nido, the "prepared adult" acts as a dynamic Montessori environment architect. Core strategies include: (1) **Environmental Minimalism** to enhance Object Permanence (e.g., peekaboo with soft blocks), (2) **Causal Modeling** via slow, visible cause-effect actions (rolling a ball to trigger sound), and (3) **Boundary Setting** through consistent routines. The adult’s role is to observe, simplify, and model—not instruct. Critical window for spatial cognition (e.g., nesting cups) and pre-math foundations (size gradients).

    1) **客体永久性训练**：通过开放式杯子喝水时的"溢出"意外（如水滴或溅落），让婴儿无意间体验"物体消失与重现"的规律，潜移默化锻炼逻辑思维。

  - 🇬🇧 **English**:
    Montessori emphasizes infant development (0-12 months) through **sensory exploration and independent movement**. Three core principles extracted:
    1) **Object Permanence**: Spills from an open cup (drops/overflow) unintentionally teach babies the concept of "disappearance and reappearance" of objects, fostering early logic.
    2) **Cause-Effect Learning**: Repeated actions (e.g., tilting the cup) help infants deduce relationships between behaviors and outcomes, forming pre-math foundations.
